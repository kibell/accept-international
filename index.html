Submit Documents to SNHU

To receive your SNHU I-20 to study in the U.S., you must first send the following documents to the intgrdocs@snhu.edu:


  • A copy of your passport
  • A bank document that shows sufficient funds and dates back no more than 6 months from today (Find out the necessary minimum balance here.)
  • An affidavit of support (if your bank document is in someone else's name)


Upon issuance of your I-20, you will receive an official accptance letter to bring with you to your visa interview.

If you are bringing dependents to the U.S., you should provide your immigration official with copies of your dependents:

  • Passports
  • Birth certificates (children)
  • Marriage cerificate (spouse)
